About StoryLand

StoryLand is an interactive children's storybook platform built as a college project, designed to make reading magical, fun, and accessible for every child. We believe every child deserves a world of stories that sparks their imagination.

Our platform features beautifully animated stories with sound effects, smart age-based filtering, and progress tracking for parents — all built with pure HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, and MongoDB.

✨ Key Features

  • 🔐 Parent Registration & JWT Authentication
  • 📚 Story Library with Age, Genre & Level Filters
  • 🎬 Animated Story Reader with Character Animations
  • 🔊 Web Audio API Sound Effects (no external files!)
  • 🎉 Confetti & Celebration on Story Completion
  • 📊 Reading Progress Tracking per User
  • 🛡️ Admin Panel for Story & User Management
  • 📱 Fully Responsive Design
